## English version

### What it is
**ScamShield for Parents** is a free web page. When an older person sees a health
article, supplement ad, suspicious text or link they're unsure about, they paste
it in. The tool gives a **conservative** risk read and writes a short message
they can **forward to their adult children** to confirm.

### Why it's needed
In the UK, **fraud is one of the most common crimes**, and older adults are
disproportionately targeted. Chinese-speaking elders are doubly stuck: limited
English, often on WeChat — UK tools don't reach them, and tools from China can't
either.

### How to use it (3 steps)
1. Open the link and paste in what you saw
2. Read the result: **looks okay / be careful / very likely a problem**, plus
   "what not to do now"
3. **Forward to family** in one tap; for scams, call the bank line **159** directly

### Privacy (important)
- No sign-up, no name, no login
- **Never enter** card numbers, verification codes or passwords
- Your history is visible only in your own browser
- See the in-app Privacy page for details
